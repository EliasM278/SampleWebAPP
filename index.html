    Who We Are

    We are one of the leading companies in Lebanon in supplying the electrical market with more than 20 world-class brands such... as Famatel ,AVe ,IDE ,BPT ,Vemer ,PCE Merz ,Telergon CNC and Megaman etc.. In addition to managing a distribution network of over 700 large, medium and small-sized electrical stores all over Lebanon ,Mge’s local targeted market also includes the industrial sector and the leading electrical, consulting ,contracting and engineering firms.Our geographical reach extends further to Other Middle Eastern regional markets such as Jordan ,Emirates,Lybia ,Egypt  etc..
